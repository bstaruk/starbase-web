Starbase

Bare metal front-end website boilerplate featuring Webpack 5, TypeScript, PostCSS & Tailwind CSS; preloaded with the modern necessities and ready for liftoff on Jamstack hosting providers including Vercel, Netlify and AWS Amplify.

Start building with one command:

npx starbase@latest
